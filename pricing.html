Pricing

Compared to most other professional web development businesses, we try to keep prices low. While you could pay thousands of dollars for a large company to create a website for you, we understand that that is a huge investment. We’ve made our prices lower so you can have a quality website without having to break the bank.

Service Price
Development $25 per page
Design $35 per page
Both $50 per page
Hosting (you must pay for your domain name!) $10 per month
Upkeep (includes changes to text, changes to code, etc.) $10 per month

So, according to our chart, a website like ours would cost you only $200, plus a $20 monthly fee for hosting and upkeep. If you’re looking to make the leap onto the web, or if you’re ready to graduate from your template website, then look no further. Our business was made for people just like you.
